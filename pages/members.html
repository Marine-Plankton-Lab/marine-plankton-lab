Sunju Kim

김선주 교수

Professor, Department of Oceanography · PKNU

Prof. Kim Sunju is a marine biologist specializing in biological oceanography, with expertise in the taxonomy, ecology, and molecular biology of marine microorganisms and protists. Her lab investigates parasitic protists in Korean coastal waters, the ecological regulation of harmful algal bloom species, and the physiology of mixotrophic dinoflagellates. She has published 36 peer-reviewed studies in journals including Nature Microbiology, Molecular Ecology, Harmful Algae, TOXINS, and Journal of Eukaryotic Microbiology.
